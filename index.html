Southwest Florida

More waterfront than Miami, still undiscovered.

Four hundred miles of navigable canals. White sand barrier islands minutes by boat. The Gulf of Mexico at your doorstep. Cape Coral sits inside the Gulf Coast triangle that Latin American and European families have chosen for three generations — discreet, low-traffic, within an hour of Naples and a short flight from Miami.

The corridor has never carried the noise of Miami or the volume of Naples. That is the point. The families already here are the ones who chose privacy over visibility.

The property

Forty-eight residences on Lake Shadroe, with the Gulf just minutes away by boat.

"Every component was positioned so the water is visible from every vantage point — resident balcony, pool chaise, restaurant table, or marina slip."

The Complete Waterfront Campus. The marina slips wrap the waterfront curve. The pool and beach zone sit directly adjacent. The Table flanks the pool with indoor dining and an outdoor terrace. The kayak launch meets the water's edge. The resort tower rises behind it all, with Lake Shadroe opening west toward the marina and the Gulf beyond. The Dalal is a marble steam bath spa designed for cleansing, relaxation, and socializing.
